Around Us

A game similar to the classic childhood game "hide n seek" just with a few twists to spice up the gameplay.

Hiders or Hunters

When loading into a match there will be players picked at random to become the hunters, and the rest, hiders.

Hunters Objective

Hunters main objective will be to find and eliminate all hiders before the hiders complete all of their tasks.

Hiders Objective

Hiders main objective will be to complete all of their tasks without being eliminated by the hunters.

Hiding

Hiders also have the ability to transform into the props around them, this will assist them in completing their tasks and make the hunters job more difficult.

And Thats It

You can also customize the settings in-game to make it harder or easier for the hunters to spice things up a bit.
